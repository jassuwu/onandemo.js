## neko (the default)

the classic cat sheet (`src/presets/neko.gif`, 256×128, 32 cells of 32×32) is
the oneko bitmap lineage as arranged by
[adryd325/oneko.js](https://github.com/adryd325/oneko.js).

- the oneko distribution the art descends from declares itself **public
  domain** — its Japanese README and the LSM file both say so — as recorded by
  [Debian's copyright file for the oneko package](https://metadata.ftp-master.debian.org/changelogs/main/o/oneko/unstable_copyright)
  ("Copyright: 1990 Tatsuya Kato … License: public-domain") and by the
  [FSF Free Software Directory](https://directory.fsf.org/wiki/Oneko)
  (License: public-domain, verified by Debian 2014-06-01).
- the pixel art is commonly attributed to Kenji Gotoh's 1989 NekoDA and
  reportedly declared public domain by him — repeated here **as reported**: no
  primary statement from Gotoh was found, so the attribution is not presented
  as established.
- the oneko distribution's non-cat sprites are **not** public domain — the BSD
  daemon (© 1988 Marshall Kirk McKusick, all rights reserved) and the
  CARDCAPTOR SAKURA characters (CLAMP / Kodansha) are carved out of the grant —
  and none of them are bundled here.

## soldier & slime

`presets/soldier.png` (Soldier-Blue) and `presets/slime.png` ship unmodified
from **Puny Characters** by Shade (Merchant Shade) —
<https://opengameart.org/content/puny-characters> — license **CC0**, stated on
the OpenGameArt page ("License(s): CC0") and on the itch.io page: "LICENSE
(CC0) — feel free to use this for your game (commercially or not) … no need to
give me credit." credit given anyway: thanks, Shade.
